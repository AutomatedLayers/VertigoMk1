## Assembly Notes

 - **2:48** You may use some force to push the Pin Holder assembly into place. You'll know it's seated because the retaining screw in the next step will thread in all the way.
 - **3:27** This module's vertical position will be adjusted in the Checks and Calibration Section.

{: .important}
Ensure the M2.5 Dowel is pushed into the pin holder all the way, and that it rotates smoothly and returns repeatably by spring force to its locating grooves.
