### 1. Assemble and Install the Filament Cutter
 - Press the **M2.5x60 Dowel** into the Pin Holder
 - Press a **Bearing - 625** on both sides of the Pin Holder
 - Push the Pin Holder assembly into the Filament Cutter Frame Mount until the bearings are seated in their pockets 
 - Use an **M3x25 SHCS** to secure the Pin Holder assembly, threading it into the plastic of the Filament Cutter Frame Mount
 - Place an **M3x16 SHCS** through one end of the **Dual Hook Tension Spring** and slide the other end onto the dowel
 - Thread the spring's M3 screw into the Filament Cutter Frame mount. The dowel should rotate out and return without resistance
 - Use 2 **M5 Roll-In Nuts** and 2 **M5x10 BHCS** to attach the Filament Cutter to the inside slot of the front vertical frame extrusion on side B. **The vertical position will be adjusted in the Checks and Calibration Section**
